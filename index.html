FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before starting.

Do I need to know coding?
Not at all. Your entire store runs on a Google Sheet. If you can edit a spreadsheet, you can run your store. Adding a product = adding a row. Changing a price = changing a cell.
How do customers pay?
Customers can pay via UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm) using the auto-generated QR code, or Cash on Delivery. The money goes directly to your UPI ID — we never touch your payments.
Is there a commission on orders?
No. Zero commission. No transaction fees. 100% of the order amount goes to you. We only charge the monthly plan fee (and the Free plan is truly free).
Can I use my own domain?
Yes, on the Pro plan. You can host your store on yourstorename.com. On Free and Starter, your store lives on our subdomain.
What happens to my data?
Your data lives in YOUR Google Sheet, in YOUR Google account. You own it completely. If you stop using StorePro, your data stays with you. We never have access to your payments or customer data.
How is this different from Shopify or Dukaan?
Shopify costs ₹2,000+/month and requires learning a complex dashboard. Dukaan charges commission. StorePro starts free, runs on Google Sheets (you already know how to use it), and charges zero commission. It's built specifically for local shops.
